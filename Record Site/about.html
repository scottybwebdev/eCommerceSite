About


This website is an example of an e-commerce website with elements of PayPal implemented. The design of the website has been created using Bootstrap. The idea of this mini project came from one of my seminars at University which I chose to expand on a little.

There are a few links I am aware of that do not work and these are intentional. They are there as placeholders as this is a demo site. Examples of this are being unable to access any of the albums on the Arctic Monkeys page apart from Suck It And See, being unable to access any of the artists from the drop down menu apart from Arctic Monkeys, and the search function not searching.

I also highly advise not entering any PayPal details as this is a demo site. Warnings are added to every accessible album.
